Frequently Asked Questions

You got questions. We got answers.

What makes Kasdava Master a "barefoot" shoe?

A zero-drop sole keeps your heel and forefoot level, while a wide toe box lets toes spread naturally. You feel the ground for better balance and posture, without sacrificing protection or grip.

Will the wide toe box look bulky?

No. The design is minimal and stylish while giving your toes the space they need. From above it looks streamlined, but inside it feels spacious and comfortable.

I have bunions / plantar fasciitis, are these shoes suitable?

Yes. The wide toe box reduces bunion pressure, and the zero-drop design encourages natural foot positioning that can ease plantar fasciitis strain. As always, consult your podiatrist for personal guidance.

How do I choose the right size?

Click "Size & Fit Chart" above. Because the design accommodates natural foot spread, order your usual shoe size. The flexible design ensures a secure, comfortable fit.

Are they slip-resistant?

The non-slip outsole is designed for wet surfaces, city walks, and indoor tile. They're grippy but still flexible for natural movement.

What materials are used?

Breathable mesh upper, flexible sole construction, and textured footbed with non-slip rubber outsole. No rigid inserts or overbuilt arch support, everything moves with your foot naturally.

Is there a warranty?

Six-month manufacturing warranty covering stitching, sole separation, and hardware defects. Normal wear-and-tear or misuse isn't covered.
